About

Built for curious minds

NovaSci Lab is an open virtual laboratory designed to make science accessible to every student — regardless of school resources, location, or language.

We believe great science teaching is hands-on, interactive, and personalised. So we built a lab where you can break things, restart, and explore at your own pace.

  • Free for all students & teachers
  • No installation — works in any modern browser
  • Privacy-respecting: your work stays on your device
  • Aligned with NCERT & state board curricula
